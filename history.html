Iconic Ferrari Moments

The Schumacher Era - 2000-2004

Michael Schumacher's partnership with Ferrari created the most dominant period in team history. Five consecutive championships established a new F1 benchmark.

The F2002 and F2004 are considered among the most perfect racing cars ever built.

Lauda's Miraculous Return - 1976

Just six weeks after his horrific Nürburgring crash, Niki Lauda returned to race at Monza. His courage became legendary in motorsport history.

Räikkönen's Last-Gasp Title - 2007

Kimi Räikkönen overcame a 17-point deficit with two races remaining to snatch the championship in one of F1's most dramatic finales.
