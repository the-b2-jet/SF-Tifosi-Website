Latest Ferrari News

Hamilton's Epic Brazil Recovery Drive - November 18, 2025

Lewis Hamilton delivered a masterclass at Interlagos, climbing from P8 to secure his second podium with Ferrari. The seven-time champion made crucial overtakes in the final laps to beat both Red Bulls.

"This SF-25 is coming alive," Hamilton said. "We're building something special here at Ferrari."

Leclerc Dominates Mexico Qualifying - November 11, 2025

Charles Leclerc claimed his fourth pole of 2025 in Mexico City, edging out Max Verstappen in a thrilling qualifying battle. The Monegasque driver put together a perfect lap when it mattered most.

"This one feels special," Leclerc commented. "The team deserves this after all the hard work."
